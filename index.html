Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh thrives in innovation through microfinance, renewable energy,digital payments, and startups.

Sustainability

Bangladesh is working to achieve
sustainable development by addressing climate change, and poverty

Infrastructure

Bangladesh's infrastructure development has helped open up the country's domestic and international trade.

Empowerment

Bangladesh has made significant progress in women's empowerment, including in education, labor force participation.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability.
With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to
enhance connectivity and access to services.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
